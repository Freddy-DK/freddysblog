Yesterday, I saw a post on LinkedIn from one of my connections, talking about moving from Wordpress to GitHub. Unfortunately, I have been unable to figure out who, else I wanted to say thanks!

![](/assets/images/2026-07-22-moved-my-blog-to-github/2026-07-22-08-03-34.png)

And, yes..., I join the apparently 100s of people moving off Wordpress to either Lovable, GitHub or other platforms with the help of AI. I found myself with $85 worth of Fable 5 credits, which had to be used for something right, so why not put it to the test.

There's probably still some cleanup to do, but I am fairly amazed with the result after a few re-run, Fable 5 was able to read the blog posts, the comments, the subscribers and all the images and push these to my new blog repository: https://github.com/Freddy-DK/freddysblog.

I am using **Jekyll** for deployment, **giscus** for like/dislike/discussions, **follow.it** for subscriptions and **VS Code** for writing. In VS Code, I have installed **eliostruyf.vscode-front-matter**, **mushan.vscode-paste-image** and **yzhang.markdown-all-in-one** to make the experience great.

Let me know if you encounter any bugs or problems with my new home.

BTW, I still have $5 left.

Enjoy
